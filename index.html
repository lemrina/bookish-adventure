Approach

Observe carefully. Validate rigorously. Communicate clearly.

Good exploration decisions sit at the intersection of field evidence, analytical discipline, and useful communication. The process below reflects how gabb.ro frames technical work: from geology and data through to interpretation and decision support.

01 Observe

Start with geological evidence from mapping, logging, and sampling rather than forcing a model too early.

02 Validate

Test the analytical dataset through structured QAQC review before placing too much weight on the numbers.

03 Interpret

Integrate geology, structure, assay trends, and spatial context into a working exploration narrative.

04 Communicate

Translate findings into technical reports, visuals, and summaries that decision makers can actually use.
